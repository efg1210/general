Things Fall Apart Essay

Click to veiw in a new page

This is an essay for English class, completed December 6th, 2016. We were to write an essay about how Okonkwo, the main character in Things Fall Apart by Chinua Achebe, was a victim of his virtues. I selected this artifact because I did well on it. It was well written, and I explained my thoughts clearly. I learned the how helpful teacher feedback could be when writing this essay, and I am thankful my teacher, Ms. Burke, looked over parts of it and helped my condense my (obviously) verbose writing. This essay shows my skills in essay writing, part of the reason I selected it. If I could do something differently, it would be to make sure the whole thing is written in the past tense. If I had done that, I would have lost only half of what I did lose.
