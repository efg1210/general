World Literature

Teacher: Ms. Burke

In World Literature we read stories and books from all over the world, and we evaluated them. It was a very interesting way to learn about other cultures, and reading in general is a fun experience. We also learned some grammar and how to write essays.

  • Research Paper
  • This is a research paper done in English class, and we were to select a topic (not in the USA or in England) and research.
  • Things Fall Apart Essay
  • This is an essay for English class and we were to write an essay about how Okonkwo, the main character in Things Fall Apart by Chinua Achebe, was a victim of his virtues.
