Connect Four

Instructions

Each player takes turns trying to get four of their disks together. Each disk is a different color, and in this game player one is pink and player two is blue. Press one of the buttons at the top and the disk the color of the player will go down the column to the first aviable space. The player that gets four disks of their color in across, up/down, or diagonally wins. Press the button below to play, or press the back button to go back to the home page.
